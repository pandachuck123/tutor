TUITION HERO

What We Do

Tuition Hero is a full service home tuition agency. We aim to help students achieve learning excellence by matching them with the right
professional tutors to suit their learning needs.

Our tutors provide learning environment that is conducive, by conducting classes in one-to-one or small group formats. This ensures
each student is nurtured with adequate attention based on their learning pace, maximising their potential to thrive.

Our goals and values

Connect the best tutors to students, provide best guidance in student’s learning pace and needs.

Reliability – We curate a tutor list based on understanding parents/students’ request on their preferable teaching methods.

Innovation – We continuously help to manage parents/tutors using a designated model to ensure quality assurance.

Efficiency – We try our best to match our client’s expectations with the right tutor in shortest possible time.
